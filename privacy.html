Privacy Policy

EFFECTIVE DATE: JULY 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how YCA Sports, LLC (“YCA Sports”, “we”, “us”) handles information in connection with this website (ycasports.com) and the mobile applications we publish. We aim to collect as little personal information as possible.

1. Scope

This policy applies to ycasports.com and to mobile applications published by YCA Sports, LLC, unless a specific application provides its own privacy policy. Where an application has its own policy, that policy governs the use of that application.

2. Information we collect on this website

This website is informational. It does not require an account, does not include contact forms, and does not set marketing or advertising cookies.

  • Technical data. Our hosting and content-delivery provider (Cloudflare) may process basic technical information — such as IP address, browser type, and request logs — to deliver the site securely and reliably.
  • E-mail. If you contact us at developer@ycasports.com, we receive the information you choose to include in your message and use it only to respond to you.

3. Information collected in our mobile applications

Each mobile application we publish discloses the data it collects in its App Store or Google Play listing and, where applicable, inside the application itself. In general, our applications collect only the information needed to provide their features, and we do not sell personal information.

4. How we use information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and applications;
  • To respond to inquiries and provide support;
  • To comply with legal obligations.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate our website and applications (for example, hosting and infrastructure providers), or where required by law.

6. Data retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above, and then delete or anonymize it.

7. Children

Our website and applications are not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The current version will always be available at ycasports.com/privacy, with the effective date shown at the top.
